About

Hi there! I’m Caroline Omland, an avid backpacker, lover of the ukulele, and a VT fashion student. This past spring break, I traveled to the nation of the Haiti to work with an organization called feedONE and I would love to tell you about it!

Throughout that week, I experienced the HOPE of the Haitian people and learned the many ways feedONE was making an incredible impact in Haiti.

Did you know that feedONE served over 19,179,753 meals last year in Haiti alone? Or that 1,850 farmers were trained? BUT, there are over 100,000 children left on a Waiting List, waiting to be fed.

It’s only $10 to feed a child for an entire month. YOU can help reduce the Waiting List.
